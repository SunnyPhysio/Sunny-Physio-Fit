Why This Matters

When the system is dysregulated,
everything is harder.

The nervous system governs how we think, connect, respond under pressure, and recover. When it's chronically dysregulated through stress, sedentary work, or accumulated physical tension, the effects reach far beyond individual wellbeing.

Productivity drops. Sick days rise. Teams fragment. Leaders become reactive. The physical and the psychological are not separate, they are one system.

Sunny Physio Fit brings evidence-based physiotherapy and nervous system regulation directly to your organisation, addressing root causes rather than symptoms, creating lasting, measurable change.

About

The person
behind the practice.

Sunny Sandhu is a Head Physiotherapist, Clinic Director, and founder of Sunny Physio Fit, a specialist B2B physiotherapy practice focused on nervous system regulation and organisational health.

Holding a degree in Physiology (BSc) and a second degree in Physiotherapy (BSc), Sunny brings a decade of clinical experience treating both musculoskeletal complaints and stress-related conditions. His background spans high-performance sport, including a career as a professional MMA fighter, through to corporate health and wellbeing.

This combination of physiology and physiotherapy training informs a practice that takes the whole system seriously. Sunny's work focuses on the interaction between physical complaints and the physiological effects of chronic stress, with particular specialisation in nervous system regulation and its role in recovery, resilience, and absence prevention.

Sunny is also a certified Functional Breathing Instructor through the Oxygen Advantage programme, integrating evidence-based breathwork interventions into clinical practice as a core tool for nervous system regulation.
